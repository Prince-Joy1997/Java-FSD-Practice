Fresher Training

Welcome to the Fresher training Program at UST. This will be a 16 week program that is divided into 2 parts.
First part is the 10 week long foundation training program and the second part is the 6 week long spcialised training program.

Capstone Project

The capstone project is a project that you will be working on for the entire duration of the training program. You will be working in a team of 4-5 members and will be mentored by a senior developer. The project will be a real world project that will be used by the company. You will be working on the project from the requirements gathering stage to the deployment stage. You will be working on the project in an agile environment.
